After his literal goddess of a girlfriend has her memory of him erased, a mortal student must try help her remember him while battling a divine fugitive responsible for the mind-wipe - a rebel ultimately determined to upset the very foundations of Creation itself.
It has been three spring-times since Belldandy came to Earth to grant Keiichi one wish. Things seem to be fine with them while living in the temple with Belldandy's sisters Urd and Skuld, but little do they know that a fallen god from Belldandy's past has escaped from his lunar prison. He has plans for everyone which do not include what the goddess and her human lover wanted for themselves.
A young man and his Angel bride must stop an angry Archangel from destroying Earth by proving that love is the ultimate force in the universe.
