Two brothers -- Ranvir and Rajvir along with their parents move to Mumbai. They endure numerous hardships, but their will to survive prevails and they succeed. Soon, their parents pass away, leaving young Rajvir in the care of Ranvir. Now wealthy beyond any means, Ranvir runs the business and Rajvir lives recklessly. When Rajvir falls in love, Ranvir arranges his marriage, but to his dismay finds out that he has arranged his brother to marry the girl he loves.
Rajvir, Ranvir, and their parents decide to re-locate from their small village in Punjab, to big city Bombay. They endure numerous hardships, but their will to survive prevails and they succeed - starting with a small dhaba, they progress on to a five star hotel. The parents pass away, leaving young Rajvir in the care of his responsible brother Ranvir. Now wealthy beyond any means, Ranvir runs the business and Rajvir just lives recklessly and without a care in the world. Rajvir falls in love, and Ranvir arranges his marriage, and then later finds out that he has arranged his brother to marry the girl he loves.
